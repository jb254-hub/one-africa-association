Why Waste Connection Matters

Across Africa, millions of tons of waste are produced every day, but less than 20% is recycled or reused. Poor waste management threatens our environment, our health, and our future. But if we connect the dots‚Äîyouth, communities, governments, businesses, and innovators‚Äîwe can change the story.

Our Core Areas of Focus

1. Community Waste Solutions

  • Educating communities on waste separation and recycling.
  • Setting up local collection hubs to make waste disposal easier.
  • Organizing clean-up drives in towns, villages, and cities.

2. Youth Empowerment Through Green Jobs

  • Training young people in recycling, upcycling, and waste management enterprises.
  • Supporting youth-led startups that turn waste into products.
  • Creating opportunities for green employment.

3. Business and Industry Partnerships

  • Working with companies to adopt eco-friendly packaging.
  • Supporting industries to set up waste collection and recycling chains.
  • Promoting circular economy models where nothing goes to waste.

4. Innovation and Technology

  • Encouraging smart waste solutions like recycling apps and digital platforms.
  • Supporting innovators who create new uses for waste materials.
  • Connecting startups with investors who want to back green technology.

5. Policy and Advocacy

  • Working with governments to strengthen waste management policies.
  • Advocating for bans on harmful single-use plastics.
  • Promoting public awareness campaigns on sustainability.
